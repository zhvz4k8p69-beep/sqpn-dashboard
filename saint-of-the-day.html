Saint Bridget of Sweden
Religious
Widowed mother of eight, Bridget founded the Bridgettine Order and spent her last years in Rome pressing for reform against Church corruption. She died in Rome on July 23, 1373 — this same date — and in 1999 was named a co-patroness of Europe alongside Saint Catherine of Siena and Saint Teresa Benedicta of the Cross.
