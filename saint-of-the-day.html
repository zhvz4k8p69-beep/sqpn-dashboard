Saint Junipero Serra
Priest
A Spanish Franciscan born in Mallorca in 1713, Serra left behind a career as a university theology professor to work as a missionary, walking hundreds of miles through Mexico before founding nine California missions beginning with San Diego in 1769. Pope Francis canonized him on September 23, 2015 in Washington, D.C., the first canonization on North American soil.
