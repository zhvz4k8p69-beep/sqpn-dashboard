Monday, June 29, 2026
Solemnity
Saints Peter and Paul
Apostles
Peter, first on every list of apostles, and Paul, who transformed from fierce persecutor to the Church's greatest missionary after his encounter with the risen Jesus on the road to Damascus, both died as martyrs in Rome around 64 AD.
