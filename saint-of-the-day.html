Optional Memorial
Saint Augustine Zhao Rong and Companions
Priest and Martyrs
Augustine Zhao Rong was a Chinese soldier who escorted a French bishop to his execution in Beijing, then converted and was later ordained a priest himself. He was martyred in 1815, one of 120 Chinese martyrs killed between 1648 and 1930 and canonized together by Pope John Paul II in 2000.
