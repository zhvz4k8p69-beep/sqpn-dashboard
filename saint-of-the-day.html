Friday, July 3, 2026
Feast
Saint Thomas
Apostle
"My Lord and my God!" Thomas said it after refusing to believe the other apostles had seen the risen Christ, then putting his own hand into Jesus's wounds. It's the most direct declaration of faith in the New Testament, and it's why we call him Doubting Thomas.
Source: John 20:28, today's Gospel
