Saint Apollinaris
Bishop and Martyr
As the first bishop of Ravenna, Apollinaris was beaten and driven out of the city for his preaching, exiled twice, tortured, and shipped off toward Greece — yet he kept returning, reaching Ravenna a fourth time before dying of wounds from a final beating in the suburb of Classis.
